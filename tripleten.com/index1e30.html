Complete FAQ
Coding bootcamps—also known as tech bootcamps, programming bootcamps, IT bootcamps, etc.—are job training programs for the digital age. Faster, more focused, and more affordable than a computer science degree. More structured, richer, and with far more support than massive online courses or free resources. A good bootcamp gives you all the skills and knowhow to get your first career-track job in tech, even if you're starting from scratch. TripleTen's boot camps differ from most it training bootcamps because they're part-time programs meant for working adults.
Most of our programs offer real-world industry experience. You'll join a team of students from different programs to work on a project for a client company. This means that you'll learn to problem-solve, apply the programming languages you've learned to new tasks, and manage time commitments, just as you would in the workplace.
Over 1,100 TripleTen grads got new jobs in tech. We're confident you will too!
If you don’t get a new job in tech within 10 months of graduation, we will refund 100% of your TripleTen tuition.
There are several conditions that apply to the refund:
— You must complete the program, including Career Prep and Career Acceleration program.
— You actively participate in a job search, applying to relevant positions and going through interviews.
— You need to be a U.S. resident.
More details can be found in the Terms of Use.
Our mission is to prepare you for your job search using the highest standards and best practices in career coaching:
  1. You’ll learn how to write a compelling resume and cover letter.
  2. You’ll graduate with a show-stopping portfolio of real-life projects that demonstrate practical work experience.
  3. We’ll help you present your best self in tech and behavioral interviews.
  4. We’ll train your soft skills and make sure you feel confident in your new position.
  5. We’ll search for positions and openings that are relevant to you.

We're thrilled to offer our graduates post-offer support through career services, designed to help them succeed in the tech industry. Students can utilize three career coaching sessions per year, depending on their needs:
  • If they've recently transitioned into a new role.
  • If, at any point, they decide to return to the job search, we'll be more than happy to set them up for success by providing career advice and reviewing their career materials.
