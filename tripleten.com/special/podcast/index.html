Thinking about breaking into tech? Changing your career can be challenging and scary. The good news is that you don't have to do it all alone. Our show will help you navigate the rocky path towards a profession in tech. Along with host Will Newsom, you'll explore the realities of shifting careers and hear real-life success stories from TripleTen students. This show is a co-production of TripleTen and Libo/Libo.
