FAQ

Complete FAQ
TripleTen bootcamps cost between $4,900 upfront for BI Analytics and $9,700 upfront for Software Engineering. Financing or installment options will bump that up, depending on the terms offered by the installment plan or financing partner.
We want all of our students to be confident that they’ve made the right choice. Students can withdraw with a full refund, up to 2 weeks after their bootcamp start date.

After 2 weeks have passed, you can still withdraw for a partial refund, up to halfway through your program. For more details and the refund schedule, check out our Terms and Conditions. They’re pretty straightforward and transparent. After receiving a refund request, we will contact you within three business days to confirm the refund amount. Once confirmed, refunds may take up to 30 calendar days, depending on the payment system.
If you don’t get a new job in tech within 6 months of graduation, we will refund 100% of your TripleTen tuition. There are several conditions that apply to the refund: 

— You must complete the program, including Career Prep and Career Acceleration program. 
— You actively participate in a job search, applying to relevant positions and going through interviews. 
— You need to be a U.S. resident. 
More details can be found in the Terms of Use.
