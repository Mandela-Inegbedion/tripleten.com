You will learn how to perform analytics using the following software tools:
  • Google Sheets spreadsheets
  • SQL
  • Tableau
  • Power BI
You will also learn the following professional skills and concepts:
  • Business concepts, such as revenue/sales, costs/expenses, profit margin, and ROI to identify opportunities for improvement.
  • Building financial models in spreadsheets to calculate profitability for different scenarios.
  • Constructing marketing and product funnels in spreadsheets to track performance over time.
  • Building cohort analyses in spreadsheets to calculate retention and churn rates based on different time periods and behaviors.
  • Calculating user activity, user sessions, LTV, and CAC to prioritize resources and identify opportunities for improvement.
Just 4 months!
You will work on projects based on real-world business tasks, such as:
  • Spreadsheets: analyze AirBnB data for the vacation rental market in Manhattan to figure out which property types to invest in.
  • SQL: understand passenger preferences for a Los Angeles-based rideshare startup, along with the impact of external factors on rides.
  • Business analytics: Build a conversion funnel for an e-commerce company, build acquisition cohorts based on users purchase dates, track monthly cohort metrics, and calculate retention rates.
  • Tableau: review department store operations and increase profitability to avoid bankruptcy.
  • Storytelling with data: help a department store CEO understand why customers are returning their purchases and what to do about it.
  • Power BI: use data scraped from publicly-available Shopify websites to analyze the app landscape on the Shopify platform, then figure out what key factors drive the success of a Shopify app.
